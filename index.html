Our struggle to be happy

This journey start a year and half ago. When i met the one i knew i was going to marry one day. I was working on a pipeline In Wyoming, which is far from my home of Louisiana. I was just discovering my desire to be a programmer when i met Alysha. I knew that fate had brought us together. Had no idea why and i was not the one to believe in these type of things at all. But without a doubt this was true. Also i was faced with medical issuses that forced me to quit pipelineing in Wyoming. Not knowing if it was altitude or what was causing me to have terrible nose bleeds.

I brought Alysha with me and we lived in Louisiana for a Year. After that Year Alysha and i went back to Wyoming she was going to visit family for a week and i was going to return to Louisiana. On my way home with two others to help me do the driving, this was a 2600 mile trip, we was pulled over and arrested after coming out of Colorado. One of us in the car had purchase legal Marijuana and brought across Texas lines. I was kept in a Texas jail for a month. Away from my finance and not working our funds had become none. With me getting released and being closer to my home thats where i went after my release. Since the we have been plagued with bad luck and seems fate has kept us apart.

I ask and pray to god for an answer as to why bring me together with my love and to hold us apart. All the while of spending money to try and keep her off the streets, which i have been a failure sometimes, i've been trying to start college as web devloper. Not an English major, as you can tell. My babe and me love each other and want nothing more but to be together, home, and happy.
